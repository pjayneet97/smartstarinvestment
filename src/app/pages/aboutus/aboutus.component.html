Smart Star Investment is a Micro Investing start-up. SSI aim is to bring the inexperienced investor in the market through SSI Pocket Application which will have investment plans. SSI helps the individual investor to choose an investment plan based on risk appetite. SSI Pocket helps your money to grow your money accordingly. By making investing simple and painless, micro-investing platforms can help an individual to invest regularly. These platforms take tiny amounts of money, usually from rounding up transactions and invests them. Small savings can add up over time to yield returns that beat traditional savings.

Why SSI Pocket

SSI has unique features which help and encourage inexperienced or even experienced individuals to invest in small amounts. invest with no minimum amount at a low cost.

In the digital era, SSI pocket helps your spare change to grow over a period of time. Each time you make a purchase, the platform rounds up your purchase to the nearest amount and deposit the difference into an investment account.
